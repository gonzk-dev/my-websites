Welcome to our club, stranger!

This is the humble website of the Visual Novel Club, a club that aims to share our love for the niche video game genre that's often overshadowed by its more flashy counterparts.
"What is a visual novel," you ask? A visual novel is a genre of game that combines the interactive and visual elements of videogames with the complex and thorough writing and prose you'd see in the books in your local library. Although the genre initialized in Japan, it has since fleshed out and expanded to many regions across the world, as the genre is easily approachable for storytellers and artists.

Overview

This club aims to unite everyone who enjoys visual novels, whether it be creating or reading them, all in one central hub. You can also share recommendations for visual novels of all genres: horror, romance, thrillers, anything! Although visual novels are niche, they are incredibly diverse. Think of this like a sillier version of a book club.
